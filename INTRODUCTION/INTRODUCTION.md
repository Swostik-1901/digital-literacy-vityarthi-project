
# Project Overview and Scenario

As a newly selected Student Digital Ambassador , your primary mission is to support your peers in navigating the complexities of the modern digital landscape.this role requires you to champion essential skills, ranging from building professional online identities to ensuring safe and responsible communication.

To demonstrate your expertise, you are tasked with creating a Digital Literacy Portfolio. This comprehensive assignment involves completing five distinct tasks—one for each course module covering topics such as infographic design, professional networking, coding collaboration, email etiquette, and cybercrime awareness. 

All project deliverables, including screenshots and checklists, must be organized within a single GitHub repository. The final step is a formal written report documenting your work and personal reflections. This project serves as a practical toolkit to help you and your batchmates thrive in a digitally-driven academic and professional environment.
